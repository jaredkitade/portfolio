The Loeb Fellowship

Branding+Web Design

The Loeb Fellowship is a global, expanding network of accomplished professionals whose work focuses on advancing positive social outcomes and revitalizing communities. This new website highlights Loeb’s focus on critical social and environmental issues through the addition of Loeb Stories. These longform articles showcase the Fellows work revolving around climate change, affordable housing, equality, and opportunity.

Alongside the website, we designed a brand new, dynamic identity. It starts as a base mark but can be adapted to suggest new ideas. It can be used with photographs, illustrations, patterns and colors to showcase different pieces of the Fellowship.

This project also included additional print and digital collateral.
