Watch The Throne

Colored Pencil | 11"x17" | 2012

"We're gonna skate to one song and one song only..."

The card is inspired by the artwork for Kanye West and JAY Z's 2011 album, Watch the Throne. The gold background was the cover art, while images of the American flag adorn the interior, reflecting lyrics about opulence, fame, and burdens of success in America. Like an actual playing card, this piece can be viewed upside-down as well.

As I mentioned above, the gold background took forever to create. Like an idiot, I thought a gold colored pencil would be enough. I had no idea that I would be using two different browns, three different yellows, a couple reds, gold, grey, and orange to make the different shades of gold in the background. If you're going to be drawing something gold, just know what you're getting yourself into.
