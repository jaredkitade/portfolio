Jared Kitade

Artist+Designer

Hi! I'm Jared. I'm an artist+designer based in the Bay Area via Sacramento. Before going to school for design, I was originally going to focus on painting and drawing.

I graduated from California College of the Arts in 2017 with High Distinction. Currently, I'm working at MendeDesign and taking freelance projects. Previously, I've worked at Retronyms and have freelanced with the Golden State Warriors, Under Armour, Hypebeast, Wells Fargo, and more.
