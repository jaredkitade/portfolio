MDES Showcase

Poster+System Design

I was asked to design a series of posters and web graphics for CCAâ€™s inaugural MDES Showcase, the final show for graduate students completing their Masters of Design in interaction design.

I had to do some research in order to learn what exactly Interaction Design was. I interviewed my roommate, who was one of the students involved in the program. He explained that, to him, Interaction Design has many facets but they all involve thinking about user interaction. It could be anything from app design to board games.

I used these ideas to help form my design. The posters were inspired by the idea of multiple facets coming together and also digital/virtual buttons.
