Sound Pack Covers

Album Cover Design

This series of Sound Pack covers was designed for AudioCopy, the largest royalty free music store on iOS. AudioCopy parters with Akai Pro, Arturia, Korg, and Loopmasters to provide professional quality sample packs to DJ’s, producers, and music makers. The packs can be used in various iOS apps, including iMPC Pro 2, Hook, Garageband, and more.

In order to create the different covers, I listened to a short 1-2 minute instrumental that was created using the samples found in the pack. I was asked to match the pack’s vibe and design something that would grab a DJ’s attention. The only criteria was that the artwork had to work as both a circle and square image.

Thank you to John-Paul and the rest of the Retronyms team for the opportunity!
