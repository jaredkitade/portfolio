Remotely Remote

Web Design

I worked with a group of senior Industrial Design students to design and code a website for CCA’s 2016 Industrial Design Senior Show. The show was called Remotely Remote and the themes were human interaction and interconnectivity. The students running the show wanted a website that reflected both of these ideas while, at the same time, a site that was unique and interesting to navigate.
